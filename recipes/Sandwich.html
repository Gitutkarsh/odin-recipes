Description

This classic grilled-cheese-sandwich is a quick breakfast prepared in minutes.

ingredients

  • White Bread
  • sliced Cheese
  • Butter
  1. Step1 ~ Gather all ingredients.
  2. Step2 ~ Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat.Generously butter one side of a slice of bread.
  3. Step3 ~ Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese
  4. Step4 ~ Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  5. Step5 ~ Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
  6. Step6 ~ Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted.
